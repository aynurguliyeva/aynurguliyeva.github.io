Tomorrowland BI Project

Analyzing festival data to derive actionable business insights.

Project Overview

This project focused on creating a full Business Intelligence pipeline to analyze Tomorrowland festival data from 2021 to 2023. The goal was to transform raw data into a clean, queryable format and visualize it to provide key business insights to stakeholders.

My Contributions

  • I designed a star schema with fact and dimension tables to structure the data, including financials, visitor behavior, and sales.
  • I used tools like Qlik Sense and QlikView to build interactive dashboards and reports.
  • I created low-fidelity prototypes in Figma to validate dashboard designs with stakeholders before implementation.
  • I performed scatter plots, area charts, and time series analysis to identify trends and patterns in the data.
